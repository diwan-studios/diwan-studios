About Us

Break the Rules says it all. Fun, creative, and out of the ordinary — we thrive on bringing the unexpected to every project we take on.

Founded in the early 2000s, Diwan Studios is the vision of Amr Diwan, whose passion for graffiti goes beyond art; it's about storytelling. With ever stroke, he infuses a unique personality into his work, whether expressing his own vision or bringing a brand's identity to life.

Since its inception, Diwan Studios has captivated audiences through the power of iconic imagery. Our designs can be found across Egypt, various cities in the Middle East, and even parts of Europe. But for us, graffiti is more than just wall art—it's a cultural narrative, a reflection of history, an statement of individuality.

Today, Diwan Studios is recognized for its high-quality, custom visual aesthetics. Every collaboration leaves a lasting impact, breaking the rules of convention to create designs that people don't just see—they feel and remember.

Mural art (Graffiti) is our core service. We specialize in working on any interior space, carefully considering the wall material and using high-qualit paints to ensure the best results. Our goal is to meet the client's objectives and exceed expectations with every project.

Mission:

We aim to help individuals focus and boost productivity in their business and endeavors by providing vibrant and inspirational services that encourage creativity. Our high-quality approach stimulates focus and drives results.

Vision:

We strive to develop communities by connecting people through art, fostering collaboration and creativity that enriches both individuals and their surroundings.
